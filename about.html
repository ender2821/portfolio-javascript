About Joshua Jensen

I am a creative with a background in both illustration and communication design. I use both areas of design in harmony creating a unique blend of a functional layout with a one of a kind illustration that draws attention to the product or service being advertised. With experience working in a technical and engineering business, I can offer the next generation of marketing materials that can sell products geared towards function over form. I have a mastery Adobe creative suite, specializing in digital painting, print layout, large format print layout, web design, studio photography and photo cleanup, photo manipulation, and user experience design.

Furthermore, I work well in a team environment; able to self-motivate and work parallel to other designers and developers on a large scale project. Working from consumer analysis to conceptual ideation all the way to a final product, I have a deep understanding of the design process to bring the most effective creative solutions to your company in this ever changing and demanding market. I take on new challenges, being able to learn software and processes on my own, and am always looking to further education. All and all, I can bring your business into the competitive market optimizing your profitability with sleek and modern creative solutions.
